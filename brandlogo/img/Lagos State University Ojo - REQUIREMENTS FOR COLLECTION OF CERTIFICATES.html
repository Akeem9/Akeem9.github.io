REQUIREMENTS FOR COLLECTION OF CERTIFICATES

 

The following requirements listed below are needed by graduands/graduates of the University for the collection of certificates:

 

The requirements for collection of Certificate[s] is highlighted as follows:

i.                    Submission of Original Notification of Result;

ii.                  Submission of Original Student’s Final Year Clearance;

iii.                Submission of Student’s Identification Slip;

iv.                Submission of Student’s Statement of Account/Evidence of payment for school fees;

v.                  Submission of Receipt/Evidence of payment for Certificate [N5000:00]; and

vi.                Evidence of payment for Convocation Brochure; and

vii.              Submission of Receipt/Evidence of payment for Alumni [N1000:00]

 

REQUIREMENTS FOR COLLECTION OF CERTIFICATE[S] BY PROXY

i.                    Submission of Photocopy of valid international Passport Data Page;

ii.                  Submission of Photocopy of valid Visa Page of International Passport;

iii.                Submission of Photograph of the graduate and proxy;

iv.                Submission of Notification of Result;

v.                  Submission of Student’s Final Year Clearance;

vi.                Submission of Student’s Identification Slip

vii.              Submission of Student’s Statement of Account/Evidence of payment for School fees;

viii.            Submission of Receipt/Evidence of payment for Certificate [N5000:00];

ix.                Evidence of payment for Convocation Brochure; and

x.                  Submission of Receipt/Evidence of payment for Alumni [N1000:00]

 

Graduands/graduates who have applied or willing to apply for their certificates are advised to check at the Certificate Unit, Administrative Block I, Room 4, Main Campus, Ojo, Lagos.
